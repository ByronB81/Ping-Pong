Ping Pong Pandemonium

How it works

Ping Pong Pandemonium will count up to the number you enter with the following exceptions

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "Ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced by "Pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "Ping Pong Pandemonium"
